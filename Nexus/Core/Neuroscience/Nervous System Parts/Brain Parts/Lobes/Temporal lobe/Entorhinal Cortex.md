Connected to [[Allocortex]] in medial [[Temporal lobe]]
Network hub for [[Memory]], navigation and perception of time

Main input and output between [[Hippocampus|hippocampus]] and [[Neocortex|neocortex]].

Forms cognitive maps together with the hippocampus.
![IMG_3385.jpeg](img_3385.jpeg)

## Medial Entorhinal Cortex

Concerned with the "where" information.

Contains grid cells that hexagonally tile physical and abstract spaces
![grid_cells.png](grid_cells.png)

This information is later fed into the hippocampus to light up the right [[Place Cells]].

## Lateral Entorhinal Cortex

Integrates the downstream processing of all sensory information into a coherent "what" understanding for memories and sends it over to the hippocampus, which together with "where" information from the medial EC makes the complete picture of the episodic memory.

[This video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9qOaII_PzGY) from [[Artem Kirsanov]] goes in detail about cognitive maps and EC and hippocampus.
