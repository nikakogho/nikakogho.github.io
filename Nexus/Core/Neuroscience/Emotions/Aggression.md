## Neural Circuit For Aggression

Cerebral cortex -> [[Amygdala]] -> [[Hypothalamus]] -> [[Periaqueductal Gray Matter (PAG)]] and [[Ventral Tegmental Area (VTA)]] -> aggressive behavior
