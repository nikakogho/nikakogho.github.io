A [[Neural Diseases|mental illness]] where the patient has an abnormal sense of reality along with loss of function. Could be more than 1 disease we are not sure.

Positive symptoms (something that shouldn't be there):

* Auditory and visual hallucinations
* Delusions
* Messy speech
* Disorganized or catatonic behavior

Negative symptoms:

* Emotional "flatline"
* Less speech
* Harder to plan and go through with plans
* Memory problems

Genetic factors determine your predisposition to it
