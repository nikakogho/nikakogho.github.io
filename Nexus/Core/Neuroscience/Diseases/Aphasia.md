Impairment of [[Neuroscience of Language|language]]

## Broca's Aphasia
When [[Broca’s Area]] gets damaged the patient can't speak/write

## Wernicke's Aphasia
When [[Wernicke’s Area]] is damaged the patient can't comprehend speech/text

## Conduction Aphasia
Both areas work fine but they are not linked anymore so patient can't repeat what he heard
