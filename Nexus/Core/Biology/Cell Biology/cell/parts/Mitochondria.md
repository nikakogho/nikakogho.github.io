Uses [[ATP]] to give energy to cell.

Was once a bacteria that invaded the cell, turned out to be useful and stayed.

Produces ATP by [[Cellular Respiration|cellular respiration]]

![mitochondria.jpeg](mitochondria.jpeg)

Has outer membrane, inner membrane, intermbrane space between them, and mitochondrial matrix inside inner membrane.
Inner membrane holds cristae (folds that increase surface area so more [[Protein|proteins]] can bind)

![mitochondria_cristae.jpeg](mitochondria_cristae.jpeg)

Mitochondrial matrix also handles [[Electron Transport Chain]]
