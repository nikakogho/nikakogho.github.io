The only company in the world making extreme ultraviolet (EUV) [[Photolithography|photolithography]] machines (needed for 2-7 nm chips) and sells to chipmakers like [[TSMC]], Samsung, Intel (and maybe eventually [[Tesla's Terafab]]).

Workings described by Veritasium [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MiUHjLxm3V0).

Based in Netherlands, and probably the ultimate bottleneck for how many watts of AI compute can be built as discussed in this [Dwarkesh podcast episode](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mDG_Hx3BSUE).

Projected to set AI compute upper limit at extra 200 GW per year by 2030.

In 2026 they make 60 EUV tools per year, expected to scale to 100 per year by 2030
