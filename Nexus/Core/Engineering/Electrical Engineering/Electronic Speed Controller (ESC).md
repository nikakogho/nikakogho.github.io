Controls speed, direction and braking of [[Electric Motor|electric motor]].

VESC is a common ESC design that is sensored.

## Sensored vs sensorless
Sensorless figures out how much to move the rotor based on [[Electromotive Force|back EMF]], which is easier at high speeds but inconsistent at low speeds and dangerous at rest since there is no back EMF and sudden start can give whiplash or break equipment.

Sensored measures at all times and gives a more continous sine wave of how much to apply at any given time.
