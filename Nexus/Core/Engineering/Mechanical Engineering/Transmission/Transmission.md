Transmission of [[Power]] from one system to another, also transforming properties along the way.
Used a lot in [[Engineering]] because [[Motor|motors]] typically use high speed and low [[Torque|torque]] but we often instead need low speed and high torque, and transmission lets us make that speed-torque tradeoff for same amount of power.

Uses [[Gears]], [[Bearings]]...

Efficiency η between 0 and 1 (how much energy is kept after losing some to friction). Lost power typically shows up in reduced torque.
