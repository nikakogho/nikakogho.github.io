Machine that pulls or lifts by winding a rope around a rotating drum (called spool).
![winch.png](winch.png)

It converts rotation of a [[Motor|motor]] into linear pull.
For a drum radius *r* and [[Torque|torque]] *T*, the pulling force is
$F = \frac{T}{r}$

## Multi-Layer Winding

Often the rope builds up in layers on top of earlier rope, which increases the radius of the winch, so the pulling force decreases the more rope you rewind. This can be avoided if you have a single-layer winding but winches usually do multi-layer.
