EMF

Work done per unit charge, measured in volts.

Produced by [[Battery]], [[Generator]] or changing [[Magnetic Field]].

Like a voltage supplied by a source.

Pushes current and is used for instance in [[Electric Motor|electric motors]] so that the current will then induce a magnetic field, apply force with it, create [[Torque|torque]] and drive motion.

Video on it [here](https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=shJAV59NS6k).

## Back EMF
Like friction but for electricity. Creates [[Voltage|voltage]] that opposes its supply, so basically acts like inefficiency factor that reduces [[Curren|current]] flow.
It is proportional to angular velocity $\dot{\theta}$:
$V_{emf} = K_t * \dot{\theta}$
