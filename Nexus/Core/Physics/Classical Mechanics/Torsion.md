A type of [[Mechanical Stress|mechanical stress]] caused by opposite [[Torque|torques]] acting on object's longitudinal axis (axis on which it's long)

Twisting a towel is everyday example of this.

## Axisymmetric

If the object is axisymmetric (symmetric on longitudinal axis) then torsion wont change its shape.
