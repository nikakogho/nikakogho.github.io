One of the fathers of [[Computer Science]].

Most famous for [[John von Neumann Architecture]] ([[CPU]] + [[RAM]] separately, talk to each other through bus) that most computers run on.

Other contributions:
- Founded [[Game Theory]]
- Had idea of [[Von Neumann Probe]] to send out self-replicating probes to colonize space
- Advanced the field of [[Quantum Mechanics]]
- Part of the [[Manhattan Project]]
- First numerical weather forecasts
