Founder and leader of modern [[Transhumanism]].

Behind the [[Philosophy of Extropy]].

Leading figure in [[Cryostasis|cryonics]], was CEO of [[Alcor]] 2010-2020.

Wrote the [extropian principles](https://www.mrob.com/pub/religion/extro_prin.html) in 1998 promoting radical enhancement.

[Interview](https://youtu.be/Ak9NsM61Xg0?si=WYalhRVoQ1DhLL3n).

He says he used to be into New Age, astral projection, occult, joined the Rosicrucians for a while, then eventually stopped believing in those but kept the inspiration of transcending the human limitations.
