Invented (or rediscovered) [[Reinforcement Learning]] while he was a PhD student of [[Andy Barto]].

Author of a popular book on RL called "Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction"

Author of [The Bitter Lesson](http://www.incompleteideas.net/IncIdeas/BitterLesson.html), notion that general purpose computational methods that can scale with more compute and with more self-play with the world will outperform specialized hand-crafted methods.

Yet he does not think that [[Large Language Models]] will scale to [[AGI]], as he says in this [interview with Dwarkesh](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=21EYKqUsPfg).
