Needs serious isolation because earth’s magnetic field, power lines and such things have stronger magnetic field than human brain.
Very expensive but high detail
![meg_device.jpeg](meg_device.jpeg)

Resolution:

* space - 2-5 mm (better than [[Electro Encephalography (EEG)|EEG]]’s 1-2cm but worse than [[Magnetic Resonance Imaging|fMRI]]’s 1-2mm)
* time - 1 millisecond (equal to EEG, better than fMRI’s 1-2 seconds)
