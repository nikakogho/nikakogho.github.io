Organization aiming to empirically figure out and constantly adjust the maximally effective use of money, time, and effort for good.
Started in 2011 by [[Toby Ord]] (author of "Precipice") and [[William MacAskill]] ([[AI Alignment]] researcher)

They keep track of multiple fields:
- Existential risks, mostly AI safety
- World health
- Animal welfare

[Website](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/)

In close collaboration with [[80 000 Hours]] and [[Foresight Institute]]
