Most common method for [[Genetic Engineering|gene editing]] is CRISPR-Cas9.
Needs a delivery method (often a recombinant [[Adeno Associated Virus]]).

Uses guideRNA to find the right spot and a Cas [[Protein|protein]] to perform the job (usually cutting [[DNA]]).

Can

* Add a new gene
* Disable an existing gene
* Make changes to an existing gene
