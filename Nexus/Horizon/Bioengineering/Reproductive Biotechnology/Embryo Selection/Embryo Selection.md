Using [[IVF]] to get many possible embryos, then doing [[Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD)]] to pick the best one and implanting that one in the mother's uterus.

Currently PGD can give us tips on avoiding disease. It is hypothesized that eventually we may uncover polygenic traits useful for [[Human Augmentation|human augmentation]], such as ones related to intelligence.
