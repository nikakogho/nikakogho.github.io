In-Vitro Fertilization.

Process to collect multiple possible eggs from mother and sperm cells from father for artificial insemination.
Most common form of [[Reproductive Biotechnology]].

## Steps

### 1. Collecting Eggs

* Ovarian Stimulation - use hormones to make the ovaries bring multiple (5-20) eggs to maturity instead of the usual 1
  ![ivf_1.png](ivf_1.png)
* Ovum Pick-Up - collect the matured eggs
  ![ivf_2.png](ivf_2.png)
* Freeze the collected eggs
  ![ivf_3.png](ivf_3.png)
* Repeat this cycle as many times as needed to collect as many eggs as desired

### 3. Fertilize The Eggs

Each egg gets fertilized with some sperm, either naturally or guided with ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)
