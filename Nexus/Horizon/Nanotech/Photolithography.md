Photons used to etch patterns. Used by [[ASML]] to make the extreme ultraviolet (EUV) tools it sells to chipmakers like [[TSMC]] to make 2-7 nm chips.

This is an example of [[Atomically Precise Manufacturing (APM)]].

Workings described by Veritasium [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MiUHjLxm3V0)
