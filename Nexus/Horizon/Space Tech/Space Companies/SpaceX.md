[[Elon Musk]]'s space company, most famous for making reusable rocket boosters for [[Falcon 9]].
Founded in 2002 with the goal of [[Mars Colonization]].
They have more flights than every other space company combined.
In 2025 world had a total 342 flights, 170 of which was from SpaceX.

Rockets:
- Falcon 9 - first reusability success in 2015
- [[Falcon Heavy]]
- [[Starship]] - in development, meant to eventually replace Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy and to be first fully reusable spaceship with expected 10 000 flights per year

Satellites:
- [[Starlink]] - satellite internet meant for entire world. Currently 8-9k satellites in [[Earth Orbits|LEO orbit]], planned to scale up to 40k
- Plan for 100GW+ [[Orbital Data Centers|orbital data center]] running [[Grok]]

Crew:
- [[Crew Dragon]] for carrying people, first used in 2021 to carry [[NASA]] astronauts
