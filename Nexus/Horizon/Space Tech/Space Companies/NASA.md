Original space agency behind big stuff like
- [[Moon Landing]] with [[Saturn V]]
- [[International Space Station]]
- [[Space Shuttle]]

And some kinda abandoned works like
- [[Space Launch System]]

Originally led by [[Verner von Braun]].
Now led by [[Jared Isaacman]].

Has a contract with [[SpaceX]] to return to the moon and establish a permanent base as part of [[Artemis III]].

## Crewed Flights
Used to rely on Space Shuttle, but it retired in 2012. After that, NASA astronauts were carried mostly by the Russian [[Soyuz]], until in 2021 SpaceX's [[Crew Dragon]] started working.
