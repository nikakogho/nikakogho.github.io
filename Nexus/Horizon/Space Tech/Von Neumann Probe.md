Concept of a self-replicating space probe that will go out into space, mine asteroids/planets and use those materials to make copies of itself that go out and do the same.

Single one could transform the galaxy within a few million years and reachable portion of [[Observable Universe]] in some billions of years.

Since we exist, this has not been built in our galaxy yet as of a million years ago, which might mean that there are no advanced aliens.
