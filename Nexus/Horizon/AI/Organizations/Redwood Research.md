[[AI Safety]] organization mostly focused on [[AI Control]].

Led by [[Buck Shlegeris]] (CEO) and [[Ryan Greenblatt]] (Chief Scientist).

Notable papers:
- [AI Control](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.06942)
- [Ctrl-Z: Controlling AI Agents via Resampling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.10374)
- [Alignment faking in large language models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.14093)
- [Sleeper Agents: Training Deceptive LLMs that Persist Through Safety Training](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.05566)
