[[AI Safety]] organization in London and San Francisco, mostly around [[AI Safety Evaluations|evals]].
Started in [2023](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/FG6icLPKizEaWHex5/announcing-apollo-research).

* [Website](https://www.apolloresearch.ai/)
* [Science of Scheming](https://www.apolloresearch.ai/science/science-of-scheming/)

Their work is around developing a Science of Scheming, and around building what they call AGI Safety Products: useful tools for AGI safety that also make money.

## AGI Safety Products

### [Watcher](https://watcher.apolloresearch.ai/)

Monitor for [[Claude Code]] to skip permissions safely and escalate the suspicious ones. Very similar in spirit to the [[AI Control]] agenda of [[Redwood Research]].
