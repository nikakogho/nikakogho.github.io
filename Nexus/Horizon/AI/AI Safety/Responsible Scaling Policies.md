## At Anthropic

AI Safety Levels (ASL):
![IMG_1404.png](asl_rsp.png)

Claude Opus 4 was first to get ASL-3.
No model has ASL-4 as of May 2026.

Originally Anthropic had a pledge in their RSP that if capabilities got too dangerous and safeguards were not in place then they would stop training and releasing models until safety side caught up, but in 2026 they updated the RSP so that they don't actually pause anymore, with the excuse that other labs are also not pausing and Anthropic isn't that much ahead of others.

They also raised the bar for what counts as AI R\&D 4 from "AI can do junior AI researcher's job" to "AI lets us do 2 years worth of research in 1 year"
