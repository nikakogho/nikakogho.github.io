AKA biocomputing

Growing mini brains in a lab to act as a computer

Explained [here](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/science/articles/10.3389/fsci.2023.1017235/full)

Feats so far:
- [Playing Pong](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9ksLuRoEq6A)
- Doing [[Natural Language Processing]] tasks
- [Playing Doom](https://medium.com/@JacksonAAaron/scientists-taught-brain-organoids-to-play-doom-d16f803412f2)

[[Cortical Labs]] is a company renting organoid intelligence chips on the cloud.

Some of them may be sentient, raising ethical [worries](https://singularityhub.com/2025/12/25/five-year-old-mini-brains-can-now-mimic-a-kindergarteners-neural-wiring-its-time-to-talk-ethics/)
