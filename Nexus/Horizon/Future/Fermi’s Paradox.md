Where are all the aliens?

* Everywhere else life gets wiped out before it reaches interplanetary level of technology
* Simply no one intersecting our [[Light Cone]] so far
* [[Dark Forest Theory]] - aliens are hiding from predator/exterminator civilizations
* Conspiracy theories that aliens are already here
* Simulation theory explanations that life is only on Earth
