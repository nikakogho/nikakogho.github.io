In addition to [[Life Improvement]], the human body and mind can directly be augmented in many ways.

Main prospects of this are:
- [[Genetic Engineering]]
- [[Embryo Selection]]
- [[Uploaded Intelligence|Mind Upload]]
- [[Nanotechnology]] used as artificial immune system
- [[Neurotech|Brain-computer interfaces]] for
	- Boosting intelligence
	- Upgrading self-control through [[Neurofeedback|neurofeedback]] or more advanced methods
	- More direct control of devices and robot bodies
	- Increasing input/output bandwidth
- [[Reverse Aging]]
